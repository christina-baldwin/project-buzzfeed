Which Fast Food Burger Reigns Supreme in Stockholm? We Tried Them All!

Max

Sweden’s pride and joy, Max isn’t just another burger chain—it’s a national treasure. With juicy patties, next-level sauces, and sustainability creds, Max is the fast food spot that actually makes you feel good about your choices. Their Friscobröd? Iconic. Their dip selection? Unmatched. If you haven’t had Max at 2 AM, have you even lived?

★

Rating: 4.8/5

Visit Max

Burger King

Flame-grilled goodness, that signature smoky taste, and fries that are criminally underrated—Burger King knows how to bring the heat. The Whopper is a heavyweight champion, and don’t even get us started on their onion rings. BK might be the underdog in Sweden, but if you know, you know.

★

Rating: 3.9/5

Visit Burger King

McDonald's

The OG. The legend. The place that turns your ‘just one cheeseburger’ craving into a full-blown feast. McDonald’s may not be Swedish, but it’s got that universal, guilty-pleasure magic that just hits right every time. Whether you’re here for a Big Mac or that elusive McFlurry that’s probably unavailable, Mickey D’s remains undefeated in sheer nostalgia power.

★

Rating: 4.0/5

Visit McDonald's

Bastard Burgers

A little pricier? Yeah. Worth it? Absolutely. Bastard Burgers is where street food meets next-level quality. Locally sourced beef, brioche buns, and that signature truffle mayo—this is the place for burger snobs (in the best way). Plus, they’ve got killer vegan options that even meat-lovers secretly crave.

★

Rating: 4.5/5

Visit Bastard Burgers

KFC

Okay, we know what you're thinking—KFC? In a burger ranking? But hear us out. The Zinger Burger is a fiery masterpiece, and if you’re all about that crispy, golden, deep-fried life, then KFC deserves a spot in your heart (and stomach). Also, let’s be real—sometimes you just need a side of popcorn chicken with your meal.

★

Rating: 3.7/5

Visit KFC
